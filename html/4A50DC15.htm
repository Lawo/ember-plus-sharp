Remarks

TMostDerived must contain a property with a getter and a setter for each child of the represented matrix. The property getters and setters can have any accessibility. The name of each property must be equal to the identifier of the corresponding child, or carry an ElementAttribute to which the identifier is passed.

The type of each TMostDerived property must be of one of the following:

  • IParameter.
  • INode.
  • IFunction.
  • IMatrix.
  • BooleanParameter.
  • EnumParameterTEnum.
  • IntegerParameter.
  • OctetstringParameter.
  • RealParameter.
  • StringParameter.
  • NullableBooleanParameter.
  • NullableEnumParameterTEnum.
  • NullableIntegerParameter.
  • NullableOctetstringParameter.
  • NullableRealParameter.
  • NullableStringParameter.
  • CollectionNodeTElement.
  • A FieldNodeTMostDerived subtype.
  • A DynamicFieldNodeTMostDerived subtype.
  • A MatrixTMostDerived subtype.
  • A DynamicMatrixTMostDerived subtype.
  • FunctionTResult, FunctionT1, TResult, FunctionT1, T2, TResult, FunctionT1, T2, T3, TResult, FunctionT1, T2, T3, T4, TResult, FunctionT1, T2, T3, T4, T5, TResult, or FunctionT1, T2, T3, T4, T5, T6, TResult.
